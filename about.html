Jon Reveal the Skier

Reveal went on to ski in 15 of Miller's films, forming a lifelong friendship with the icon. He frequently shares the words of Miller is describing his love for skiing, "It's about making semi-circles on tilted frozen water with a smila on your face."

After teaching in New Zealand as a McKinsey Scholar, and the Dodge Ridge and Bear Valley in California, Reveal moved inot the ski area management in Colorado. He revamped Kestone and Arapahoe Basin and went on to serve as Vice Presicent of Operations for Aspen Skiing Company. He then joined the team that designed, built and operated Yellowstone Club, the world-class, private resort in Montana.

While climbing the ranks of the ski resort industry, he also found time to climb Mount Everest-without oxygen.

Throughout his life, Reveal; has focused his energies on fostering a love and appreciation for outdoor recreation. Mountain sports-whether skiing, hiking, climbing, or biking-have always brought him so much joy, peace and freedom. Those feelings, along with great memories and long-lasting relationships are what he strives to impart whenever he interacts with Tamarack's students and guests.
